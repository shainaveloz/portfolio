About

My interest sparked while working at the Institute for Simulation and Training (IST)- NASA-gOE lab where coding became one of my responsibilities. Since then, I’ve enrolled in the first cohort of the University of Central Florida's Coding Boot Camp. As a Front End Developer, I’m proficient in HTML5, CSS3, JavaScript, JQuery, JSON API, AJAX, Firebase, GitHub, Heroku, and command line. In addition, I have completed studying Back End Development working with languages such as Node.js, Express.js, React.js, Angular.js, MySQL, Mongo DB, Handlebars.js and PHP frameworks such as Laravel where I became a Full Stack Web Developer upon my graduation in late September 2016.

I am currently working as a Front End Developer, where I am helping create a new application using technologies such as AngularJS, THREE.js, and SASS.
